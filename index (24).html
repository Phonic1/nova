Welcome to Balance Quest.

The school's Balance Meter is flashing red. Notif the Gremlin is sneaking through the school, trying to steal everyone's attention and throw screen habits out of balance.

Today, you will follow Nova through 5 stations. At each station, Nova will run into a different screen-time problem.

Your job is to help Nova choose what to do:

A = balanced choice
B = unhelpful choice

After each choice: watch the 5-second outcome video, complete the micro-mission, collect the passport code, and move to the next station.

If Nova makes an unhelpful choice, don't worry. You can still help Nova reset and get back on track.

Say together:
"Screens are a tool. We choose when to use them."
